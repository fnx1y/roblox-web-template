Central London Police Department

The Central London Police Department operates under a structured, disciplined framework designed to maintain order, realism, and professionalism. This system ensures all personnel follow defined protocols and uphold departmental standards at all times.

This platform serves as the official operational interface, providing access to rules, rank structures, and command procedures. All members are expected to remain compliant with these systems.

Rules & Conduct

All personnel must maintain professionalism at all times. Disruptive behavior, harassment, or misuse of authority will result in disciplinary action.

Communication must remain appropriate. Offensive language and bypassing filters is strictly prohibited.

Chain of Command

All matters must follow proper escalation: PO → SUPT → D/INS → INS → DC → AC → CH → Ownership

Rank Structure

Ranks define authority, responsibility, and operational control. Personnel must follow all orders issued by superior ranks unless otherwise directed.
