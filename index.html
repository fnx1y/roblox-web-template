Central London Police Department

The Central London Police Department operates under a strict and structured command system designed to ensure discipline, efficiency, and realism. This system defines expectations, responsibilities, and operational conduct for all personnel.

All members are expected to maintain professionalism at all times, follow the chain of command, and uphold the standards of the department. This platform serves as the official information hub for all operations and policies.

Rules & Conduct

All personnel must maintain a professional attitude at all times. Disruptive behavior, trolling, or immaturity is not tolerated.

Respect towards all individuals is mandatory. Harassment, discrimination, or targeting others will result in disciplinary action.

Communication must remain appropriate and professional. Offensive language and filter bypassing are prohibited.

Chain of Command

All issues must follow the structured chain of command to ensure proper handling and escalation.

PO → SUPT → D/INS → INS → DC → AC → CH → Ownership

Rank Structure

Low ranks perform patrol duties and follow orders. Middle ranks supervise and assist leadership. High ranks manage operations and enforce discipline. Chief ranks maintain full control over departmental decisions.

RoWifi Nickname Bypass

Only Chief ranks may grant permanent nickname bypass. Temporary bypasses must not exceed 14 days.

All members must include their rank within their nickname unless authorized otherwise.
