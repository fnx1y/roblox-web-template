Central London Police Department

🏠 Welcome

Welcome to the Central London Police Department, a professional roleplay experience built to renovate and influence policing within the Roblox platform.

📌 This website contains all official information including rules, punishments, rank structure, and documents. Every member is expected to read and understand these guidelines.

🎯 Our mission is to create a structured, fair, and realistic environment where players can engage in high-quality law enforcement roleplay.

📜 Rules & Conduct

1. Professionalism
All members must remain professional at all times. This includes behavior, communication, and actions in-game and within the community.

2. Respect
You must treat all players equally with respect. Harassment, targeting, or disrespect is not tolerated.

3. Language Policy
Swearing, offensive language, or bypassing filters is strictly prohibited.

4. Anti-Discrimination
Racism, slurs, or discriminatory behavior results in severe punishment.

5. Exploiting / Hacking
Any unfair advantage, scripts, or hacks will lead to bans without warning.

6. Platform Compliance
All users must follow Roblox and Discord Terms of Service.

7. Staff Authority
Failure to follow staff instructions will result in disciplinary action.

8. Roleplay Integrity
Do not disrupt scenes, troll, or break immersion intentionally.

9. Spam / Abuse
Spamming or abusing systems (radio, chat, etc.) is not allowed.

10. Common Sense
If something is clearly wrong, do not do it.

📊 Rank Structure & Duties

Low Ranks
Handle patrol duties, respond to calls, follow orders.

Middle Ranks
Assist in leadership, supervise lower ranks, maintain order.

High Ranks
Manage divisions, enforce discipline, oversee operations.

Headquarters
Administrative roles, manage systems, assist leadership.

Chief Ranks
Lead the department, make decisions, enforce standards.

Ownership
Full control of the department and all operations.

🪪 RoWifi Nickname Bypass

Only Chief ranks and above may grant permanent bypass.

Temporary bypass max: 14 days.

Rank must be included in nickname.

Symbols are restricted unless authorized.
