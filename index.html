Central London Police Department

The Central London Police Department operates under a structured and highly disciplined command system designed to ensure operational efficiency, realism, and professionalism across all interactions. This platform serves as the primary knowledge base for all personnel and outlines the expectations required to maintain order within the department.

All members are expected to familiarize themselves with departmental regulations, rank responsibilities, and proper chain of command procedures. Failure to comply with these standards may result in disciplinary action depending on severity and repetition of violations.

Rules & Conduct

All members must maintain professionalism at all times. This includes behavior, communication, and actions both in operational environments and community platforms. Disruptive or immature conduct is not tolerated.

Respect towards all individuals is mandatory regardless of rank. Harassment, discrimination, or targeting individuals is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disciplinary action.

The use of offensive language, including attempts to bypass chat filters, is not permitted. Communication must remain clear, professional, and appropriate.

Chain of Command

The chain of command exists to ensure efficient communication and structured escalation of issues. Members must report to their immediate superior before escalating further.

PO → SUPT → D/INS → INS → DC → AC → CH → Ownership

Skipping ranks without valid reason is considered a violation of protocol and may result in disciplinary action.

Rank Structure

Low ranks are responsible for patrol operations and responding to incidents while following direct orders.

Middle ranks assist in supervision, maintaining discipline, and ensuring proper execution of tasks.

High ranks manage divisions and oversee operational structure.

Chief ranks maintain full authority over departmental decisions and enforcement of standards.

RoWifi Nickname Bypass

Nickname bypass permissions are strictly controlled and may only be granted by Chief ranks or above. Temporary permissions may not exceed 14 days.

All members must include their rank within their nickname unless authorized otherwise.
