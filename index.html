Central London Police Department

🏠 Welcome

The Central London Police Department is a structured and professional law enforcement roleplay environment built to renovate and influence policing within the platform. Our objective is to provide a realistic, fair, and engaging experience where all members operate under clear expectations and defined systems.

This platform serves as the official information hub for all members and visitors. Within this system, you will find detailed regulations, rank responsibilities, and operational guidelines that govern the department. All members are expected to review this information thoroughly and uphold the standards presented.

Our mission is to maintain order, professionalism, and immersion at all times. Every action taken by a member reflects not only themselves but the department as a whole.

📜 Rules & Conduct Policy

The Central London Police Department enforces a strict code of conduct designed to ensure fairness, professionalism, and a high-quality roleplay experience. These rules apply at all times across all platforms.

Professional Conduct:
Members must maintain a professional attitude at all times. Disruptive, immature, or unrealistic behavior will not be tolerated and will result in disciplinary action.

Respect:
All individuals must be treated equally and respectfully. Harassment, toxicity, or targeting others is prohibited.

Language Policy:
The use of inappropriate or offensive language, including attempts to bypass filters, is strictly forbidden.

Discrimination:
Racism or any form of discrimination will result in immediate and severe punishment.

Exploiting:
Use of cheats, exploits, or unfair advantages is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.

Platform Compliance:
All members must follow Roblox and Discord Terms of Service at all times.

Authority Compliance:
Failure to follow staff or higher rank instructions may result in disciplinary action.

Roleplay Integrity:
Do not disrupt scenes, troll, or intentionally break immersion.

Common Sense:
Any action that is clearly harmful or disruptive, even if not listed, is prohibited.

📊 Rank Structure & Responsibilities

Low Ranks (PO → LT)
Responsible for basic patrol duties, responding to incidents, and following orders from higher ranks. They form the foundation of the department and are expected to learn procedures and maintain discipline.

Middle Ranks (CPT → CDR)
Assist in supervising lower ranks, maintaining order, and supporting operational leadership. They act as a bridge between general members and high command.

High Ranks (D/COM → COM)
Responsible for managing divisions, enforcing discipline, and ensuring operations run effectively. They oversee department-wide activity.

Headquarters (D/INS → ADV)
Administrative and structural roles responsible for internal management, coordination, and supporting executive decisions.

Chief Ranks (DC → CH)
Top-level command responsible for enforcing standards, making critical decisions, and leading the department as a whole.

Ownership
Holds full authority over all systems, rules, and operations within the department.
