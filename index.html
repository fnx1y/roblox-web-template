Welcome to the Central London Police Department. This experience is designed to provide a professional, structured, and realistic policing environment where all members are expected to follow rules, respect others, and contribute positively.

Official Rules & Conduct

Professionalism: Maintain a mature and professional attitude at all times.

Respect: Treat all members fairly and respectfully.

No Swearing: Offensive language is not allowed.

No Racism: Zero tolerance for discrimination or slurs.

No Exploiting: Cheating or hacking results in bans.

Follow ToS: Must follow Roblox and Discord rules.

Listen to Staff: Follow directions from higher ranks.
