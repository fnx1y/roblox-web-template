Central London Police Department

🏠 Welcome

The Central London Police Department is a professional law enforcement roleplay environment designed to renovate and influence policing standards. Our system is built on structure, discipline, and realism, ensuring every member operates within a clearly defined framework.

This platform acts as the official information hub for all users. It contains all policies, rules, rank structures, and operational expectations. Every member is responsible for reviewing and understanding this information before participating in departmental activities.

Our mission is to maintain professionalism, enforce fairness, and provide a high-quality immersive experience where actions have structure and leadership has purpose.

📜 Rules & Conduct Policy

Professional Conduct:
All members must maintain professionalism at all times. Immature, disruptive, or unrealistic behavior will not be tolerated.

Respect:
All users must treat others with respect. Harassment or toxicity is prohibited.

Language:
Offensive language or bypassing filters is not allowed.

Discrimination:
Racism or discrimination results in severe punishment.

Exploiting:
Cheats or unfair advantages result in bans.

Follow ToS:
All members must follow Roblox and Discord rules.

Authority:
Follow all instructions from higher ranks.

Roleplay Integrity:
Do not disrupt scenes or troll.

Common Sense:
If it’s clearly wrong, don’t do it.

🧭 Chain of Command

The Chain of Command ensures all communication and issues are handled efficiently and professionally. Members must follow this hierarchy when escalating issues or requesting assistance.

PO → SUPT → D/INS → INS → DC → AC → CH → Ownership
Rules:
• Report to your immediate superior first
• Do not skip ranks without reason
• Provide context when escalating
• Respect all decisions made by leadership

📊 Rank Structure

Low Ranks: Patrol and respond to calls

Middle Ranks: Supervise and assist leadership

High Ranks: Manage divisions and enforce discipline

HQ: Administrative roles

Chief: Lead department

Ownership: Full control
